About

Description

20 Minute Tasks strives to be a different TODO app, by adding anti-burnout measures and doing away with streaks while still finding ways to keep you motivated. The principle behind the app is simple and is what made me start improving at 5 different things simultaneously. Depending on how much free time you have and how much you want to learn/improve at, the app will calculate some achievable goals and will then help you stay on track to achieve them.

Why

I started the system behind the app on accident, when I wanted to learn new things but not be pressured to do every thing every day. The system originally started on a pdf on my iPad, but when I realized it was working extremely well for me, I started thinking about developing it into an app.
