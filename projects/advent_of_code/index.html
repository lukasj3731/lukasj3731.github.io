About

Description

Advent of Code is a yearly advent calendar, in which two tasks must be solved each day from the 1st of december up until the 25th in an effort to save christmas. As a developer, I consider it my duty to help save christmas and am therefore participating every year. I'm getting up early every day in december to solve the tasks as soon as they go live, resulting in sometimes messy code, but also sometimes leaderboard positions in the hundreds and definitely a lot of coding experience.

Why

"Why" is the question I ask myself every day during december when my alarm goes off. But when I'm up and awake, in the zone programming, and successfully solving tasks, everything is worth it. The competetiveness really motivates me and I've gained heaps of experience through these challenges.
