About

Description

An internet puzzle hunt for IT-experienced players. The puzzle starts with a simple QR code, but advances through steganography, cryptography, programming and much more. The puzzle remains unsolved to this day, although it has not yet had it's official launch. The puzzle is not based on any internet services, and is therefore safe from any such services shutting down.

Why

This puzzle was created originally to expand my knowledge on cryptography, but turned into a year-long journey through a diverse set of topics. It has been a learning experience and has resparked my love of computer science and design every time I continued working on it.
