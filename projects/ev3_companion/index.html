About

Description

EV3 Companion is a cute little robot, that makes a desk feel more alive. It's almost like a pet, although it lacks intelligence to be on even footing. It can still be entertaining to watch as they roam around the desk, avoid ledges and look around.

Why

I found 2 Boxes of EV3 Robots at work one day, and I was excited beyond belief! I've been building things with LEGO since I was little, and always wanted to try out one of these LEGO robots. I took the boxes home and had lots of fun building, programming and tinkering with this robot.
