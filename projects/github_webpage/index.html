About

Description

My GitHub website is meant to establich my online presence and be more visible to future employers as well as potential collaborators. The site showcases my general information and qualification, as well as projects I've been involved with. Every project has it's own page, with a descriprion and images to give the viewer deeper insight.

Why

As my career is advancing, I'm trying to be as visible as possible without using social media. The possibility to easily host pages on GitHub seemed perfect, since I'm active here anyways. This also gives me way more flexibility, since I can design, change and expand my page however I like without being limited by constraints typically present in social media.
